Free Vehicle Emission Testing Suddenly Stopped

By Arsalan Khattak | Published Jun 26, 2025 | 10:59 am

The Punjab government has announced that all Emissions Testing System (ETS) booths across Lahore will remain closed today due to continuous rainfall.

According to officials, ETS booths operate in open areas, and the decision was made to ensure the safety of both ETS teams and the public. Citizens have been advised to avoid visiting ETS booths today and may call 1373 for further information.

Meanwhile, Chief Minister Punjab Maryam Nawaz recently approved an extension of the free emissions testing deadline. Initially scheduled to end on June 30, the free service will now continue until August 31, 2025, allowing more citizens to benefit.
