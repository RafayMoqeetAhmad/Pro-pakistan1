Punjab University to Launch Professional Diploma Program

By Rija Sohaib | Published Jun 26, 2025 | 3:13 pm

In a significant move to enhance its administrative framework and professional standards, Punjab University has announced the launch of a new professional diploma program.

The diploma, focusing on Management Systems and Standards, will be offered under the Department of Quality and Technology Management. It aims to equip students with the skills to develop robust systems for quality, safety, and sustainability.

The university has confirmed that the diploma classes will be available both in physical classrooms and through online learning, providing flexibility to participants.
