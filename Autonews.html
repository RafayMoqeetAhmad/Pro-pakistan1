JAC Motors Pakistan Closes Bookings for T9 Hunter

By Arsalan Khattak | Published Jun 27, 2025 | 12:11 pm

JAC Motors Pakistan has announced that bookings for the popular JAC T9 Hunter will be temporarily closed starting July 1, 2025.

The company issued an official statement thanking customers for the overwhelming response to the pickup truck and confirmed that the closure is aimed at ensuring timely deliveries and maintaining high service standards.

The announcement comes on the heels of a limited-time offer that allowed customers to lock in the price of the T9 Hunter regardless of future government-imposed taxes, provided they completed full payment by June 30, 2025. JAC Motors acknowledged the strong trust and interest this offer generated among buyers

“To uphold our commitment to timely deliveries and maintain the highest standards of service, we will be concluding bookings as planned,” the company said in its public notice

The price lock guarantee will only be valid for those who made full payments before the June 30 deadline. Customers who miss the cut-off will have to wait for future booking phases, the company hinted.
